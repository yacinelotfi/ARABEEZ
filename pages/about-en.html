About Us

A simplified educational application for teaching Arabic letters with diacritics, aimed at children and beginners. It was developed to provide free and simple educational tools that help build early reading skills.

The Developer

Name: Yacine LOTFI

Email: yacine.lotfi@example.com

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to design high-quality, easily accessible Arabic language learning tools for children, parents, and teachers. We believe that learning should be fun and interactive.

Core Features

  • Interactive and fun teaching of Arabic letters
  • A variety of educational games to reinforce learning
  • Support for both Arabic and English languages
  • 100% free with no intrusive ads
