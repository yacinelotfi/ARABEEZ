1. Introduction

Welcome to the Arabic Letters Learning Application ("Application," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices, how we collect and use information, and your rights regarding your data.

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, IP address
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, interactions with content
  • Location Data: General geographic location (based on IP address, not precise GPS)
  • Referrer Information: How you arrived at our site

B. Information from Third Parties

  • Google Analytics collects usage and performance data
  • Google AdSense and advertising partners may collect data for ad personalization
  • CDN providers may log access patterns

C. Information You Voluntarily Provide

  • Contact form submissions (name, email, message)
  • Comments or feedback through contact channels

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Application and user experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and site performance
  • To deliver personalized advertisements (via Google AdSense)
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our Application. Google Analytics collects data such as pages visited, referrer, user demographics, and device information. This data is used to optimize our content and improve user experience.

Google Analytics Data Retention: 14 months (default)

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

5. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Application. Google and its advertising partners use cookies and data to:

  • Display personalized ads based on your interests
  • Track ad performance and user interactions
  • Prevent ad fraud

Google Ad Technologies: Google uses DoubleClick cookies, Google's DART cookie, and other tracking technologies.

You can control ad personalization through:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) Opt-Out
  • Your Ad Choices (Canada)

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We and our advertising partners use cookies, pixels, web beacons, and similar technologies to track your activity and preferences. These include:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Track user behavior (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising Cookies: Enable personalized ads (Google AdSense)
  • Performance Cookies: Measure site performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Although our Application is designed for educational purposes and may be used by children, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us immediately at the address below.

8. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

A. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - EU Users

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Opt out of data processing for marketing

B. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - California Users

California residents have the right to:

  • Know: What personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information
  • Limit Use: Limit use and disclosure of sensitive personal information

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Use of our Application is at your own risk.
